## Notes on Citation Standards

All citations follow Chicago Manual of Style, 17th Edition, for academic papers. In-text citations use superscript numbers corresponding to the bibliography. Direct quotes are verified against original sources where possible. Statistical claims are cross-referenced against multiple sources for accuracy.

## Fact-Checking Notes

This bibliography includes sources used to verify factual claims in the manuscript. Where discrepancies were found between sources, the most authoritative primary source was used. Government documents and official statistics take precedence over news reports. Academic research provides theoretical framework and context.
