# Tables and Figures for Austin Housing Policy Transformation

## Table 1: Major Housing Reforms Timeline (2023-2025)

| Date | Reform | Council Vote | Key Provisions |
|------|--------|--------------|----------------|
| December 7, 2023 | HOME Phase 1 | 9-2 | Reduced lot sizes to 2,500 sq ft; allowed 3 units on standard lots; reduced front setbacks to 15 feet |
| July 20, 2023 | Borden Dairy TOD | 7-1-1 | 450 housing units approved in former industrial site |
| February 29, 2024 | Density Bonus 90 | 7-3 | Up to 90% bonus for 10% affordable housing; no height limits |
| May 16, 2024 | HOME Phase 2 | 10-1 | Eliminated parking minimums; allowed up to 10 units on corner lots |
| May 16, 2024 | ETOD Overlays | 10-1 | Density bonuses near 28 transit stations; reduced parking near transit |
| May 16, 2024 | Compatibility Relaxation | 10-1 | Reduced compatibility setbacks; increased building envelope |
| July 10, 2025 | Single-Stair Buildings | Pending | Allow single-stair buildings up to 6 stories (effective date) |
